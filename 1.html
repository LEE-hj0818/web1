Vestige란 무엇인가

1. a small part or amount of something
larger, stronger, or more important
that still exists from something
that existed in the past

2. a bodily part or organ
that is small and degenerate or imperfectly developed in
comparison to one more fully developed in
an earlier stage of the individual,
in a past generation, or in closely related forms
